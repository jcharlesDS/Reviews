The crux of Animal Well lies in its densely layered [b]exploration[/b] and [b]experimentation[/b] complemented by an alluring [b]atmosphere[/b]. This synergy between discovery and mood heavily contributes to its excellence. [b]Knowledge[/b] is often the reward for succeeding, and it vastly expands the [b]depth of interactions[/b] with each item, animal, and puzzle solution. The stylized color palette and immersive sound effects add to the tension and wonder of the world. There are so many layers to this game, and yet the base content is [b]accessible[/b] to everyone. It can be as simple or complex as you want it to be. This is a passion project worth experiencing!
