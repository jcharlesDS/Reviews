Game starts out rather slow and not very interesting, it picks up after chapter 1, and gets only better.
 Content is unique along with its story, you get to control party of 4 with option to switch out members, and build their abilities, armor, weapons. You travel open world, mostly load screens when you talk, teleport and story sequences. Each companion has its own short story.
Can buy the game dirt cheap and its worth it.

Pros
- zones design is good
- amount of grind is low
- music is alright
- sfx is okay
- combat difficulty can by adjusted mid game
- option to min max characters with crystals and gear
- crafting is decent ( armor, weapons, utilities, crystal
- quests are rather simple, they are there to help you level and make some extra money
- main quest is cool along with its story
- leveling up is rather quick
- world bosses are cool looking
- save anywhere option
- plenty places to teleport across the continent
- didnt encounter game breaking bugs
- cool mount early on

Cons
- some texture glitches
- audio can get little glitchy at times
- there seem to be some game content missing but its not game breaking in any way, like custom outfits
