A third-person shooter RPG set in the world of Gun Gale Online, offering a darker atmosphere and more tactical, ranged combat. For the first time in the series, you create your own character and follow an alternate story path alongside familiar SAO characters.

Gameplay centers on gun-based combat, skill customization, loot, and character builds. The story includes branching choices that affect the ending, and a separate Kirito Mode lets you play as the main cast.
Main story takes about 30+ hours.
